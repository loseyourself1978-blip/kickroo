Kickroo! turns soccer into a loud, fast, one-thumb arcade bounce battle.

Pick a country-inspired squad, warm up in Practice First, then enter Global Cup 48: 12 groups, knockout rounds, and one wild trophy chase. Swipe any teammate to send them flying into the ball. Swipe faster for more force. After contact, players bounce away, so every shot becomes a fresh flick, rebound, and scramble.

Kickroo! is built around quick cartoon collisions: a soccer ball, bouncy strikers, sideline props, goal frames, officials, and a big gold GOAL! moment when the chaos pays off.

Why players will get it fast:

- Swipe any player, not just one striker
- Fast swipes create bigger hits
- Players rebound after contact instead of camping on the ball
- 3v3 group matches, 5v5 knockout chaos, 6v6 final pressure
- Big GOAL! moments with score flashes, crowd roars, and boos
- Global Cup 48 with groups, standings, and knockout drama
- North America-inspired cartoon energy, outfits, colors, and symbols
- Quick portrait matches built for one more try
