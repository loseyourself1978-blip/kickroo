# Optional App Preview Plan

App previews are optional for this release. If a video is recorded later, use up to three short clips in this order:

1. **Swipe Any Player**: Start on a teammate, flick into the ball, show contact recoil as the player bounces away, then show a clean ricochet through players and officials.
2. **Global Cup 48**: Show the home screen, Practice First, then official cup HUD with the clear safe-area `Exit` control and group match detail.
3. **Goal Moment**: Capture a gold `GOAL!` overlay, highlighted score, announcer-style goal call, crowd cheer/boo, and restart whistle.

Recommended first submission path:

- Upload the four prepared screenshots first.
- Add app previews only after a polished gameplay capture can be recorded at an accepted App Store preview size and frame rate.
