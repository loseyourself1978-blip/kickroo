Gameplay Help

  • Swipe from any teammate to move that player.
  • Swipe faster for a stronger collision.
  • After a player hits the ball, they rebound away. Swipe the same player again if you want another run.
  • Tap Left, Roar, or Right to push the ball with crowd waves.
  • Practice First does not affect cup standings.

App Store Notes

Kickroo! is an unofficial arcade soccer game and does not use official event marks, licensed teams, real players, betting, or real-money prizes.
